Projects

The Partner

The Partner analyzes data from IRS 990 Filings for nonprofit organizations from Amazon Web Services and visually displays information about an organization's expenses, sources of revenue, and significant contributions to the community.

Dear Ally

Dear Ally is a winning hackathon project from Chime Hack 3 hosted by Facebook. The project creates a safe chat room for victims of violence, and connects them to professionals that can give them immediate assistance.

The Watch

The Watch is a winning hackathon project from Hack Against Violence hosted by Runway and DBC. The Watch scraps Twitter for tweets that match keywords that indicate users that show signs of suicide and puts them on our watch list.
